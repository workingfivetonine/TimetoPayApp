## 1. Currently live copy

### Landing page
- **Eyebrow badge:** AI-powered grocery tracking
- **Hero headline:** Turn your receipts into real grocery savings
- **Hero subtitle:** Scan any receipt and TimetoPay reads every item and price, tracks how they change over time, and builds a smart shopping list that always points you to the cheapest store.

**Feature blurbs**
- **Scan with AI** — Snap a photo of any receipt and AI pulls out every item, price, and store in seconds — no typing.
- **Track prices over time** — See how grocery prices move week to week, and which store gives you the best deal on the things you buy.
- **Smart shopping list** — Your regulars are detected automatically and grouped by store, with the lowest known price next to each item.
- **Spending insights** — Weekly spend trends with high and low flags, plus full price history for every item you've ever bought.

**How it works:** Scan your receipt → We do the rest → Shop smarter
